Leadership Style

Embracing Autonomy: I advocate for autonomy and trust in my team members, favoring their ability to make informed decisions over micromanagement.

Fostering Problem Solvers: I highly regard problem-solving skills, recognizing them as essential attributes within my team, even above technical engineering prowess.
